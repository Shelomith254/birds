Doves are graceful and gentle birds, often associated with peace, love, and hope.
Known for their serene appearance, they have smooth, white feathers and soft coos that have made them symbols of purity and harmony in various cultures around the world.
Doves are often seen as messengers, delivering goodwill and representing calm in times of conflict.
These birds are typically monogamous, forming lifelong bonds with a single mate, which further symbolizes their association with fidelity and love.
Doves also play important roles in rituals, such as weddings, where they are released to signify unity and peace.
With their gentle nature and beautiful flight, doves evoke a sense of tranquility, reminding us of the importance of peace, compassion, and unity in our lives.
